Your Questions, Answered

Welcome to FAQ. Here, we have compiled answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about our construction services. If you have any additional questions, please feel free to contact us.

General Questions

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Dhaka, Chattogram, Sylhet, Khulna, Rajshahi, and surrounding areas in Bangladesh.

What types of construction services do you offer?

We offer a variety of construction services, including custom homes, excavation and foundation, available homes, and custom designs.

Do you offer free estimates and consultations?

Yes, we provide free estimates and consultations for all potential projects. Contact us to schedule yours today.

Custom Homes

Can you build a custom home within my budget?

Absolutely. We specialize in building custom homes that fit within your financial plans without compromising on quality.

How long does it take to build a custom home?

The timeline for building a custom home varies based on the size and complexity of the project. On average, it can take between 6 to 12 months from design to completion.

Custom Designs

How do your custom design services work?

Our custom design process is collaborative. We start with a consultation to understand your vision, then work with you to create a design that meets your needs and preferences.

What is the benefit of choosing a custom design over a standard model?

A custom design allows you to create a home that is uniquely yours, tailored to your specific tastes, needs, and lifestyle, ensuring complete satisfaction.

Warranty and Support

Do you offer any warranties on your work?

Yes, we offer a 1-year warranty on new home construction, providing you with peace of mind and assurance of our quality.
